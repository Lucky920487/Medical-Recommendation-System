Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App with Flask and Machine Learning

In today‚Äôs digital era, access to timely and accurate healthcare is more critical than ever. With the rise of telemedicine, we set out to build a symptom-based disease diagnosis web app powered by Flask and Machine Learning. Using a Decision Tree Classifier, our platform helps users identify potential illnesses quickly based on their symptoms.

The Problem

People often face overlapping symptoms and struggle to understand what condition they might be dealing with. Our challenge was to build a tool that provides quick, reliable, and understandable insights without replacing professional consultation.

The Solution

We designed a web app that predicts possible diseases from user-input symptoms. The system combines data-driven AI models with an intuitive UI to make healthcare more accessible.

Users enter symptoms in a simple, user-friendly form that supports a wide variety of conditions.

A Decision Tree Classifier trained on medical datasets predicts possible diseases.

The system instantly returns the most probable diagnosis, along with additional insights.

Beyond Prediction

Along with predictions, our app provides disease details, recommended precautions, medication suggestions, dietary guidelines, and workout tips. This ensures that users get holistic insights to support healthier lifestyles.
