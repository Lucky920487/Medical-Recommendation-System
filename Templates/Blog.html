Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App

Using Flask, Machine Learning, and a Decision Tree Classifier

The Problem

People often experience symptoms and want quick answers about their health concerns. It can be challenging to differentiate between diseases, especially when symptoms overlap. Our goal was to provide a convenient solution for users to input their symptoms and receive potential diagnoses.

The Solution

We developed a web app that allows users to enter a list of symptoms they are experiencing. The app then uses a pre-trained Decision Tree Classifier model to predict the most likely disease.
